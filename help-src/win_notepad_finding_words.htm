To find specific characters or words

  1. On the Edit menu, click Find.
  2. In Find what, type the characters or words you want to find.
  3. Click Find Next.

Notes

  • To find only text with uppercase and lowercase characters, as specified in Find what, select Match case.
  • You can specify which direction to search starting from the insertion point in the document. In Direction, click Up to search from the insertion point to the top. Click Down to search from the insertion point to the bottom.
